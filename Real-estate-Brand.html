Belle Monde - Real Estate Business

Website for a Real Estate company. The purpose of this website is to showcase their various projects and establish a credible Brand.
            
Interaction Design | Research | Prototyping | Branding | UX.
4 min read

Problem Statement
            

Designing a website for a Real Estate company. The purpose of this website is to showcase their various projects. It can be ongoing projects, completed projects etc...Each project will have an individual landing page connected to the website. The website has to be planned in such a way that it is best compatible for SEO activities.

Brand Idea
            

Modern architecture and design values simplicity and clean lines. Incorporating these qualities in your brand can communicate credibility, positivity, and professionalism, helping increase brand awareness and attract customers. Aligning with modernism values also communicates trustworthiness and makes securing loans easier, increasing property value and making your brand a top choice for buyers

Ecosystem
            

The ecosystem for a real estate company could consist of user from different ecosystems. When talking about buying a flat In a residential area, we can find a few different kinds of users:
1. A person who wants to buy a house for living.
2. A person who is buying a house as an investment He wants the house and area to give him maximum returns in terms of per sq. ft and also rent.
Although being a luxury brand they provide homes for every type of customer. Whether 1BHK to 3BHK to commercial space for small or big company . When we talk about commercial area although much more expensive in terms of land and rent. But companies want as much facility in terms of transport, security office essential facility and convenience. 

Approach 
            

Users often perceive aesthetically pleasing design as design that's more usable. (Aesthetic Usability Effect) With the help of this law we would create a simple yet aesthetically pleasing website with all the necessary Information about each real-estate project. Users spend most of their time on other sites. This means that users prefer your site to work the same way as all the other sites they already know. (Jacobs Law) Using some common features of other competitors we would improve on them and make our site more interactive. Elements tend to be perceived into groups If they are sharing an area with a clearly defined boundary (Law of common region). Objects that are near 0r are in close proximity to each other are tend to be grouped together(Law of Proximity). 

Geography
            

In a real estate business there is are many geographic factors that govern an on going project such as transport, supermarkets, locality. Although our projects are spread over many locations in the city of Mumbai, one thing that plays a major role the locality and area as the price per sq. ft. changes, which in turn changes the target audience.
English will be a common language for the site which makes it easier for international investors and buyers to get information about our company

Tech Assessment
            

We would prefer to host our site on and Indian server which would help our Indian customers to with better loading time great experience we can also use Google firebase as our hosting site firebase has great analytics and authentication for our site.

Although every real estate has a modern architecture but they are unable to full fill the needs of the user by not having all the Information about there project.
Thus it Is Important to give them all the necessary Information about the project. It is also tough to judge a project when it is an on going project that is it has not been completed yet.
