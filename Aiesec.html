Brief History of AIESEC 
            

AIESEC is a global non-profit organization run by students and graduates, present in 126 countries with 80,000 members. It offers its members leadership opportunities, international internships and a global learning environment to develop skills and knowledge. It is independent, non-political and aims to develop and engage young people to create a positive impact on the world.
