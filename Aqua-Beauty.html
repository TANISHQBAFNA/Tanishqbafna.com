Aqua Beauty - Salon App

Mobile app for a Salon company. The main motive of the company is to promote business during COVID-19, by creating an online presence.

Problem Statement
            

Design a Mobile app for a Salon company. The app should be compatible for IOS. The main motive of the company is to promote how special they are during COVID-19. They have their own products to be sold too. They are an elite brand. The clients come because they are the best in the industry because of their works.

Brand Idea
            

To brand this app, we should focus on highlighting the luxurious and safe salon experience that it offers during COVID-19. We can position the app as an exclusive platform for clients who value exceptional services and expertise in the beauty industry. The brand should communicate a sense of exclusivity, elegance, and quality to attract high-end clients who prioritize luxury and safety. We can also leverage social media and influencer partnerships to showcase the app's unique features and benefits, creating a buzz around the brand and driving user adoption.With our mobile app, clients can easily book appointments and select their preferred salon services. They can also purchase our salon products, which are exclusively available at our salon. Our products are carefully curated, ensuring that they meet our high standards of quality and effectiveness.

Ecosystem
            

The ecosystem for a salon is quite broad; therefore, the user base for the app will also be wide. Personal grooming for every user is essential and is always a priority whether the user is below the age of 18 or above the age of 18. 
We can find a diverse variety of users:
1. Users who visit the salon at a high frequency (2-3 times a month or higher).
2. User who visit the salon at a lower frequency (1-2 times a month).
3. User who visit the salon at a rare frequency (once in two month).
Due to the covid-19 restriction people were unable to visit the salon, irrespective of how frequently one user visit's the salon. People were unsure if the salon were safe even after the restrictions were removed. There was a certain level of doubt in the user's mind.

Approach 
            

Users often perceive aesthetically pleasing design as design that's more usable. (Aesthetic Usability Effect) With the help of this law we would create a simple yet aesthetically pleasing website with all the necessary Information about each real-estate project. Users spend most of their time on other sites. This means that users prefer your site to work the same way as all the other sites they already know. (Jacobs Law) Using some common features of other competitors we would improve on them and make our site more interactive. Elements tend to be perceived into groups If they are sharing an area with a clearly defined boundary (Law of common region). Objects that are near 0r are in close proximity to each other are tend to be grouped together (Law of Proximity).
